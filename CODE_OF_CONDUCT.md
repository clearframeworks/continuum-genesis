# Code Of Conduct

Continuum Genesis is a technical project for public AI memory infrastructure. Participation should stay clear, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Standards

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Discuss code, design, evidence, and tradeoffs.
- Respect privacy boundaries and do not request or publish sensitive data.
- Assume contributors may be using different tools, models, operating systems, or skill levels.

## Enforcement

ClearFrameworks maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior makes the project harder to maintain safely.
